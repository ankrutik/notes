# Lifting
Tags: #watercolour/techniques  

Apply paint. 
- While paint is wet
	- dry brush -> using the dry brush, lift the paint
	- put water on the part of the paint to lift -> dry brush -> now lift the water
	- 

Same when done with tissue paper, is called blotting.
