#coffee/dial-in 
### Procedure
Add 10 ml to final brew. Taste.
Repeat till you get desired taste.
### Impacts on cup
- Reduces strength for heavily processed coffee. Example [[intellect_cereza_hidden_alchemy]]
- Opens up flavours. 
- Makes brew smoother, rounder, sweeter.
