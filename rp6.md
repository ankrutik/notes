From Coffeevine mag, Volume 37, 12/2025, page 6, **Leaves** Tokyo, Japan
> **Brewer**: V60 **Dosage**: 13g Grind: **Medium**-coarse **Water**: 200g at 90 - 93 C
> 
> Pour 40g of water with a 5g per second speed and evenly draw a spiral pattern in the slurry. At 30 seconds, add 90g in the same pattern and speed. At 1:20 minute, add the last remaining 70g in a central pour. Allow everything to draw down and swirl your vessel before pouring.
