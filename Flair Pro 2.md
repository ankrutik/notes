#coffee #espresso

Takes 25 minutes total: grind, assemble, pull, clean up
# Compulsory
- Needs to be bolted
- Coffee needs to be really finely ground
	- Timemore C2 Max 
		- 9 clicks at least
		- 9 minutes to grind medium dark
	- Blue Tokai's "Espresso Grind (Commercial)" is not enough
